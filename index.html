Documented State

The Loop

A recursive psychedelic state in which thought, speech, intention, or action begin to repeat in a self-reinforcing circle. Time feels folded. Meaning feels sticky. The mind becomes trapped in a pattern it can see, but cannot easily exit.

Common Triggers

High-dose psychedelics, cannabis amplification, anxiety spikes, confusion in social settings, sleep loss, overstimulation, dissociation.

Substances Reported

LSD, psilocybin, cannabis, DMT, ketamine, and mixed states involving fatigue, panic, or overstimulation.

Typical Markers

Repeated phrases, repeated actions, returning thoughts, déjà vu, narrative collapse, “stuck” feeling, confusion about what comes next.

Grounding Notes

Reduce stimulation. Change rooms if safe. Hydrate. Slow the breath. Let another grounded person redirect attention outward and anchor the sequence gently.
